## Requirements
While a basic understanding of Flutter and the packages covered is required to make the most of the content on this website, you're encouraged to also refer to all official documentations for a more comprehensive understanding.

There's still so much more to learn and discover. I strongly encourage further exploration of other sources and the world of Flutter development. With dedication and perseverance, you can reach new heights in your development journey.

## About the Author
Shaun Wu is a bilingual Singapore-based Flutter Developer. Projects he has previously worked/is currently working on include B2B Marketplace, Healthcare Management System, Sales Management Platform, etc. He is also a mentor to a few developers who just started with Flutter.
