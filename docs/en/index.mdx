shaunwu.dev is website that shares supplementary resources on Riverpod and other Flutter packages that can be used in combination with. You will find insights, references, and "how-to" tutorials.

<Info>While a basic understanding of Flutter and the packages covered is required to make the most of the content on this website, the author encourages readers to also refer to the official documentation for a more comprehensive understanding.</Info>

There's still so much more to learn and discover. The author encourages further exploration of other sources and the world of Flutter development. With dedication and perseverance, you can reach new heights in your development journey.

## About the Author
Shaun Wu is a bilingual Singapore-based Flutter Developer. Projects he has previously worked/is currently working on include B2B Marketplace, Healthcare Management System, Sales Management Platform, etc. He is also a mentor to a few developers who just started with Flutter.
